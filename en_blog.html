How is cardiovascular health supported in China?

In traditional Chinese culture, great attention is paid to the circulation of qi and blood. Herbal components are used to support cardiovascular health.

Ginkgo Biloba

Used to support microcirculation. Included in formulations aimed at balancing the body.

Ginseng

An adaptogen traditionally used to increase the body's overall resilience.

Musk Sage (Danshen)

Used to activate blood and eliminate stagnation.

Note: Information is for cultural purposes. Not a medical recommendation.
