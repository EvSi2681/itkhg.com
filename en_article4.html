In traditional Chinese medicine, great attention is paid to the balance of Yin-Yang and the state of the nervous system. Herbal formulas are used to support the emotional state.

In China, various approaches are used to support mental and emotional health, including diet, meditation, and herbal remedies. Some modern medications, such as Sertraline, are used in clinical practice, but their use requires strict medical supervision.
